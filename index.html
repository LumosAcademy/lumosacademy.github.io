Why Lumos Academy

Enlighten every child's learning experience

In a world of worksheets and screens, we do things differently. At LAC, kids are make, move, and collaborate every single day. Our school promotes curiosity, creativity, exploration, problem-solving and social skills.

🛠️

Hands-On, Screens-Off

Every class at Lumos involves brainstorming, designing, building and collaboration. Kids work with real tools, real materials, and real challenges. As a result, they develop problem-solving, critical thinking, teamwork and project management skills.

👥

Work in Teams

Unlike most enrichment programs, every LAC class is team-based. Students learn to lead, contribute, and support each other — skills that matter far beyond school.

🌱

Life Skills That Last

Sewing, woodworking, coding, public speaking, dance, drama — we teach skills that build confidence, creativity, and capability for life, not just the next test.

FAQ

Questions parents ask us most

We know you have questions before entrusting us with your child. Here are the ones we hear most often.

What age range does Lumos Academy serve?▾
Lumos Academy serves children ages 5–14, covering kindergarten through middle school. Our curriculum is structured by age group so the content and team dynamics are developmentally appropriate for every child.
What does "team-based" learning actually mean?▾
Every project at LAC is completed as a team of 4–6 kids. Rather than each child working alone, they divide responsibilities, solve problems together, and share their work as a group. This mirrors how adults work in the real world — and makes learning much more engaging and memorable.
What is the student-to-teacher ratio?▾
We maintain a maximum of 14 students per instructor. This ensures every child gets personal attention and that teams are small enough to be genuinely collaborative.
Where is Lumos Academy located?▾
Lumos Academy serves the Tri-Valley area, with families from Dublin, Pleasanton, Livermore, and San Ramon. Our exact address will be announced before our Fall 2026 launch. Join our waitlist to be the first to know.
What happens if my child misses a class?▾
We understand kids get sick and life happens. We offer one makeup session per month for Full Month Program members. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance when possible.
Do you offer sibling discounts or financial aid?▾
Yes — we offer a sibling discount for families enrolling more than one child, and we have a limited number of need-based financial aid spots for each session. Please contact us directly to discuss what's available.
Can I cancel or change my plan?▾
Yes. The Single Class Pass can be paused or cancelled with 5 days' notice before your next billing cycle. The Full Month Program requires 10 days' notice before the next month begins.
When does the Fall 2026 program start?▾
We are targeting September 2026, following local school district calendars. Summer intensive camps are planned for July 2026. Exact dates will be emailed to everyone on our waitlist first.
